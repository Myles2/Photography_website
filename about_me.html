About me

Hi, my name is Myles and I am an experienced Photographer who is passionate about taking meaningful photos that can tell a story. Every photo I take I always try to think of ways to evoke emotions in the viewer. With todays society anyone can take a photo and a decent one at that, my goal is not to just take nice photos but photos that you can look back at and have a moment of nostalgia.

In my photography career I have photographed 3+ Weddings, 25+ Special Events, 50+ Portrait Photos 10+ Real Estate Photos, 30+ Aerial Photos, and I also have experience in videography recording over 100+ hours of video and editing them for various events.

I am also an aspiring Web Developer/Software Engineer which has helped me develop a keen eye for detail, and design.

Photos are glimpses in one’s life, sometimes they are of special moments, important memories, or even just what you ate for lunch on a random day. But in those special moments it can be very useful to have a photographer who can capture those occasions meaningfully allowing you to enjoy living in the moment. If you would like to book a session or have any questions, please contact me and I will get back to you as soon as possible.
